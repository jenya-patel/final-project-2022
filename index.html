Who We Are

REM is the stage of sleep where dreams are created. For many small businesses, COVID-19 has been an absolute nightmare. Small businesses are struggling to stay open and face significant problems attracting, engaging and retaining customers. Now more than ever, they need support.

Mission

At Rem and Company, we're on a mission to keep doors open and dreams alive. We began by offering free consulting services to small businesses impacted by COVID-19. Through industry research and working with clients, we expanded to meet the needs of our clients and grew into an information hub and digital community platform for small businesses.

Services

Marketing

Every brand is trying to get their customers’ attention right now and customers are overwhelmed. We work with each of our clients to examine their marketing strategies across various platforms and help brands develop appropriate and effective messaging and campaigns. Our expertise is not only in creating valuable content but in optimization through analytics.

Strategy

Our strategy is rooted in research and the mantra “it's not only what you know but who you know”. Our consultants are immersed in what is going on in your industry, what your competitors are doing, and everything COVID-19 related. We are here to guide and connect with the available resources out there whether they are financial, legal, or technological.

UI/UX

By analyzing our clients current operations, we think of ways to best utilize employees and preserve resources. Whether it is interviewing users, researching trends, performing competitive analyses or AB testing, our consultants are adept at analyzing data in order to strategize and test potential technology solutions.
