FAQS

Who can join?

Undergraduates of any major on campus are welcome to apply to Rem! You do not need any prior consulting experience and can apply as a freshman, sophomore, or junior.

How to apply?

Visit Rem and Company's national page and complete the application form under Rem on Campus. We will be accepting applications at the beginning of each semester.

What kind of clients does Rem work with?

Rem focuses on working with female and minority owned small businesses in Ann Arbor and Detroit. Visit our clients page to see past projects.
